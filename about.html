About Eggy Studio

Eggy Studio is focused on creating clear web tools for tradespeople, small workshops, carpenters, furniture makers and practical builders. The Wood Sheet Calculator was created to solve a common problem: estimating sheet material before cutting begins. Many carpentry jobs start with a rough list of cabinet sides, shelves, doors, backs and panels. Turning that list into a sheet quantity can be slow when the workshop also needs to consider material type, available stock, leftover pieces and waste.

This website combines practical woodworking knowledge with a simple browser-based calculator. The goal is not to replace the judgment of an experienced carpenter. Instead, it gives a fast starting point that helps users compare material requirements, prepare quotations, review stock and understand how pieces might fit on sheet goods.

The content on this site is written for clarity. We avoid confusing jargon where possible and explain workshop terms such as kerf, offcut, sheet utilization and grain direction in plain English. Our priority is to make the tool useful for beginners while still being practical for professionals who need a quick calculator.

Wood Sheet Calculator is maintained as a lightweight static website, which helps it load quickly on phones, tablets and workshop computers. Fast loading matters because many users check estimates while standing near material racks, in a store, or on a job site with limited internet speed.
