Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eggy Studio handles information when you use Wood Sheet Calculator. The calculator is designed to work in your browser and does not require account registration.

Information You Provide

If you contact us by email or through the contact form, we may receive your name, email address, subject and message. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry, improve the website, investigate technical issues or manage professional communication.

Calculator Data

Measurements, material names, stock entries and cutting requirements entered into the calculator are processed in your browser. We do not require you to submit this data to a server to use the calculator.

Cookies and Advertising

This website may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site operation, analytics and advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

When Google AdSense or related Google advertising services are used, Google may use cookies, including advertising cookies, to personalize or measure ads. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Users may also visit www.aboutads.info to learn more about choices for third-party vendor advertising cookies.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics or standard analytics tools to understand page visits, device types, browser performance and popular content. Analytics data helps us improve guides, calculator usability and mobile performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate hosting, analytics, email or advertising services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the website from abuse.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. You can also avoid sending contact information by not using the contact form. If you have emailed us and want your message removed from our records, contact hello@eggystudio.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Eggy Studio at hello@eggystudio.com.
